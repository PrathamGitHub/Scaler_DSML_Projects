Municipal Corporation Solid Waste Disposal Site Selection- Multi-Criteria Analysis, Analytical Hierarchy Process

2022 – 2022

Show project
With the rapid pace of urbanization and development, a huge amount of solid waste generated and its 
disposal is becoming a huge challenge for mankind. The land part of the Earth has become increasingly 
scarce and the decision of site selection for solid waste disposal is now facing more opposition from the public. The 
solution to this problem can get easy public acceptance if the solution is approached by following a scientific 
methodology. In the present study, the integration of Geographical Information System (GIS) and Multi-Criteria 
Evaluation (MCE) is used for the decision-making process. Mangaluru is one of the fastest developing cities in recent 
times and becoming a hub of huge waste. The lack of scientific solid waste dumping site and insufficient capacity of the 
existing open dumping yard at Konchady suggest the immediate need for a scientific approach towards site selection 
for solid waste disposal for Mangaluru City Corporation (MCC). 
 Site selection for solid waste disposal is influenced by many factors. But considering the availability of the data 
the study considers the factors: distance from water bodies, distance from the road network, distance from the airport site, 
LU/LC class and ground slope. Each factor is compared to another, and its relative importance in the selection process 
is accessed using a pairwise comparison matrix. One of the outputs of the study is a final suitability map along with 
the location and information of the most suitable site parcels. Out of the total area of study, 98.43% area is 
identified as ‘unsuitable’ for the solid waste disposal site, 0.18% as ‘moderate suitable’ and 1.39% area (7.87 
sq.km. i.e. 786.76 Ha) ‘most suitable’ site for solid waste disposal. The area identified under ‘most suitable’ 
category in this study needs further field research and cost-benefit analysis is essential before proposing the 
particular site for the solid waste disposal
